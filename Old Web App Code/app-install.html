Install Sports Hub App

Add BHS Sports Hub to your home screen for quick access and a native app experience

📱

iOS (iPhone/iPad)

  1. 1 Open Safari and navigate to this website
  2. 2 Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. 4 Customize the name if desired and tap "Add"
🤖

Android

  1. 1 Open Chrome and navigate to this website
  2. 2 Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right
  3. 3 Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. 4 Tap "Add" to confirm the installation

Benefits of Installing the App

  • Quick access from your home screen
  • Works offline for previously viewed content
  • Native app-like experience
  • Faster loading times
  • No need to remember the website URL
  • Push notifications (when available)
